This interactive comic are created to fulfill my degree project which titled " Interactive Comic as educational tool to spread dengue awareness.

One of its objective is to compare the effectiveness between the traditional method (newspaper,brochure,pamplet ) and digital method (interactive comic) in learning about new information.

 It is hoped that the reader would understand more about the information presented and taking suitable measure that can help their environment.
